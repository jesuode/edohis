Department Overview

A critical pillar of Edo State's healthcare system

The Department of Planning, Research and Statistics (DPRS) plays a critical role in supporting the operations of the Edo State Health Insurance Commission.

The department is responsible for collecting healthcare data, conducting research, monitoring healthcare facilities, and producing statistical reports that guide policy decisions and improve healthcare delivery across the state.

What We Do

Our Core Responsibilities

Five key areas where the department delivers measurable impact for healthcare across Edo State.

Research & Policy Analysis

Conducting studies and generating insights that support healthcare planning and policy development.

Data Collection

Gathering healthcare data from field operations and health facilities for monitoring and reporting.

Facilities Monitoring

Supervising accredited healthcare facilities under the Edo State Health Insurance Scheme.

Program Evaluation

Assessing the effectiveness of healthcare programs and initiatives.

Statistical Reporting

Producing data reports that support decision making within the commission.
